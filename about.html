Our Chapter

Both State High TSA chapter 9/10 and 11/12 are part of PATSA Region 8 and have participated in TSA for many years. We are located in State College, Pennsylvania. Our school routinely achieves impressive performances in TSA, sending students to States and then Nationals every year. We encourage students to engage in community service projects and operate with a "Education First" attitude.

Both State High chapters strive to follow the TSA motto of "Learning to Lead In A Technological World."

We accomplish this by working as a team at weekly practices to develop the best understanding of various aspects of STEM. In addition, we elect a President, Vice-President, Secretary, Treasurer, Reporter, and Sergeant-at-Arms to develop and practice leadership skills.

The developers of this website wish to thank Mr. Olmstead!

Mr. Olmstead is the TSA 9/10 chapter advisor at State College Area High School. Throughout our TSA experience this year, he has helped us understand what is necessary to be good people, do well in TSA, and follow the TSA motto.
